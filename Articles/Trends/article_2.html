Yoga Is The New International Trend Now!
Posted On: JULY 2 2021
By: Anonymous

ॐ त्र्यम्बकं यजामहे सुगन्धिं पुष्टिवर्धनम् |
उर्वारुकमिव बन्धनान्मृत्योर्मुक्षीय माऽमृतात् ||

We worship the three-eyed One, who is fragrant and who nourishes all.Like the fruit falls off from the bondage of the stem, may we be liberated from death, from mortality. The standard of living has grown radically in the vicinity of existence. Many people tend to misinterpret healthy standards of living with dieting, gymming, looking gorgeous and much more, but in reality it is more about the right food at the right amount of eating at the right time. In addition to this, some physical activity will witness a stress relieved mindset and acclaimed unnecessary stress as an individual experience. Due to a current COVID-19 pandemic, increased working hours and monotonous routine has caused people a very tense and stressful environment. Many people are also suffering irritation and dryness in their eyes. Therefore, people are curious to find new methods for recreational activities. For people who love scenery, traveling, exploration can be refreshing, enthralling and astonishing. Contrarily, for people who like to pamper themselves loves to go for a spa or an Ayurveda retreat. For people who love the blending of all physical and mental comfort in the process, guess what Yoga sounds best for you! In this digital era, technology advancement has resulted in the people getting scammed towards unhealthy and lethargic standards of living. Yoga, an ancient practice, again boomed as a method of coming together with a serene standard of living. It helps in adopting the healthier standard of living by incorporating brain power, organization of body and soul. Yoga turns out to be aiding people in finding their internal tranquility and serenity.

Benefits from Yoga

Since COVID-19 pandemic is at its peak, Yoga helps in building strong immunity, regulating breathing capacity and is involved in boosting an individual’s self-confidence.
This pandemic has made us work on computers for long work durations, ultimately resulting in less movement of our joints and muscles making them stiff. Yoga asanas have come out to be an incomparable way in loosening up stiff muscles and enhancing the locomotion of joints. Yoga possesses building core strength, building abdomen and muscles stronger. The asanas help in creating stronger focus and concentration.
