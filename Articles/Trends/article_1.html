Indian Fashion Trends Which Are Rocking The World
Posted On: 29-6-21
By: Prashant Kumar Jha

Indian fashion is globally accepted by people, fashion enthusiasts and even big fashion designers and houses. India is rich in Textile and textile design, many different types of art works, embroidery works you will find only in India. Foreigners are always amazed by the way Indians dress and how they carry it. We find different types of clothing all across India. In recent times, we find that the designers are heavily using the Indian textiles and putting some of the western elements into it, or they are using different design techniques and embroidery techniques and implementing them. We find big fashion houses collaborating with the Indian designers on the global level. Let’s have a look at some of these big projects!

Designer Handbags

A perfectly suitable leather handbag completes a poetry called lady. It must make an aesthetic statement matching the occasion for which it is designed. It’s true, the best handbags for women are designed for different occasions. Any negligence regarding the design- occasion combination indicates that a person shows it does not have a form of a kind but lacks the content that defines it. Indian handbags are not only famous in the country but world wide also, the designer cultural embroideries, floral designs on leather, unique textures makes it look attractive and a good way to go to all functions and parties. Indian handbag designs are cherished in the world resulting in many designers collaborating with foreign ones to create a mixture of culture and traditions and showcase it in the form of fashion trends.

Kurta- Pyjama

If we are talking about Indian fashion, we can’t forget the Kurta Pyjama trends. It is a wardrobe which is worn by ministers to ordinary citizens and truly showcases the touch of Indian-ness. No wonder that even international markets are day to day coming with their unique designs of this comfortable yet classy attire. The garment is said to have originated in the Indian subcontinent and usually has regional variations. Whether it is a family function, official ceremony or even at home, this garment is All-In-One type which is the main reason men love it. Countries such as New York, USA, France etc. have collaborated previously with some Indian fashion designers to create some wonderful textures, with the mix of both Indian and western styles and are already looking to collaborate in future too which is of course a rocking mix!

Indian Jewellery

Indian jewellers are famous across the world, a trend which has been alive for centuries. In the past kings and queens of different nations welcomed Indian artisans and craftsmen with open arms, artisans who crafted exquisite creations for them. Gifts – Jewellery is gifted on most auspicious occasions in India. Jewellery may be attached to the body or the clothes. From a western perspective, the term is restricted to durable ornaments, excluding flowers for example.
